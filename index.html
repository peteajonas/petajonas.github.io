About

I wasn't bred to be a musician, it just happened upon me. In a home full of music lovers whose vinyl record collection was too large to catalogue, the turntable was my first place to discover a love for music. My sister had a dusty electric guitar in her bedroom. That guitar slowly made its way to his bedroom. One guitar became several. Little vintage toy keyboards came into the bedroom, these became an organ, a piano, and an accordion. Since, I have expanded from playing the guitar and the keyboards to composing, film scoring, producing, and songwriting.

Music has been the backbone my life, connecting to the people who make it and the avid listeners alike, he has been able to make what I consider the most interesting and amazing web of friendships.
